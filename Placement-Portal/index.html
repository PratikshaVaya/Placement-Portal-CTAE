About the Institution

Engineering Excellence Since 1964

College of Technology and Engineering (CTAE), Udaipur is a constituent college of Maharana Pratap University of Agriculture and Technology (MPUAT). A pioneer in technical education in Rajasthan, CTAE has produced thousands of engineers and leaders who shape industries across India and globally.

Our campus in the scenic City of Lakes features cutting-edge labs, research facilities, innovation hubs, and vibrant student communities across 10+ engineering disciplines.

Recruitment Process

Step-by-Step Placement Guide

A streamlined, transparent process making hiring from CTAE effortless for companies of every size.

Step 01

Company Registration

Register on the portal with your company details, JD, CTC, and eligibility criteria. Our team approves within 48 hours.

Step 02

Pre-Placement Talk (PPT)

Conduct an online or campus PPT to introduce your organization. Significantly improves application quality and student intent.

Step 03

Resume Shortlisting

Receive applications via the portal. Filter by CGPA, branch, skills, or any custom criteria. Our T&P Cell assists in coordination.

Step 04

Aptitude / Online Test

Conduct tests on campus using our computer labs, or remotely via your preferred platform. We handle all logistics.

Step 05

Technical & HR Interviews

On-campus or virtual interviews. Dedicated rooms, breakout areas, and hospitality for your panel.

Step 06

Offer & Onboarding

Share offer letters via the portal. We manage documentation, verification, and joining formalities for selected students.
